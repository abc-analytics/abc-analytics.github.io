  • The Most Important Thing: Uncommon Sense for the Thoughtful Investor
  • Howard Marks
  • A list of important things to know as a value investor and elaboration on each point. The points are self-explanatory and universally-true, so a mere point-form list suffices.
    1. 2nd-level thinking
    2. Understanding efficiency, value, price vs value
    3. Understanding risk, recognizing it, and controlling it
    4. Aware of cyclicality and trends
    5. Know afterall timing is critical
    6. Be patient and consistent/persistent in finding bargains during market extremes
    7. Be confident but not over-confident and keep your cool/logic at all time
    8. Appreciate probability and uncertainty, so not be runined by the "impossible"
