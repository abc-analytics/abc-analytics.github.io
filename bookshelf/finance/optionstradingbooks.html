  • Options for Volatile Markets
  • Richard Lehman
  • Overly simplified and in a way misleading as it claims covered shorts are "conservative" trades while they are short put synthetics; the returns quoted are mostly best-case numbers with multiple roll-overs and assuming mild realised volatility which when explodes can be devastating. Should emphasize that option selling is just an add-on to pre-determined long/short picks with strikes selected to reflect price views. However, I do like the VIX/VXX introduction at the end, which touches upon how VXX performs relative to VIX based on vol curve shape.
